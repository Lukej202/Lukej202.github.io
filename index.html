About Us

At A-Nails and Beyond Mobile Pet Care, we

bring compassionate, professional

veterinary technician services right to your

doorstep - because pets (and their people)

deserve care that is convenient, stress-free,

and affordable.

With over 25 years of experience

each as Registered Veterinary

Technicians,

we are passionate about what we do and are

deeply committed to the health and

happines of your pets. We provide services

that include nail trims, anal gland care, ear

cleanings...and more!

Not just for dogs and cats but also for

guinea pigs, rabbits, ferrets, as well as

many other exotic pets including

reptiles with feet!

We pride ourselves on building trust with

our clients, and offering honest,

educated advice. If something doesn't

seem right, we will always let you know

and help guide you to the best veterinary

care possible.

-Sharon RVT

-Ellen RVT
